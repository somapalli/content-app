Lead Responsibilities (Onsite/Offshore)


Story Understanding and Refinement – Actively participate in refinement sessions to thoroughly understand user stories from a development and technical implementation perspective.


Architecture and Detailed Design – Develop architecture solutions and detailed design documents based on functional specifications. Ensure the design artifacts are well-documented and stored within each repository for future reference.


Business and Customer Context – Gain a strong understanding of business objectives and customer needs to ensure technical solutions align with overall product goals.


Technical Guidance and Troubleshooting – Provide technical assistance to the team for resolving complex issues, debugging, and ensuring smooth delivery.


Non-Functional Requirements (NFRs) – Define and deliver technology solutions that meet performance, scalability, availability, and SLA expectations.


Hands-On Development – Be actively involved in coding and debugging, especially for production issues or critical defect resolutions.


Vulnerability Management – Oversee and ensure timely remediation of all vulnerabilities for the repositories under your ownership, maintaining compliance with security standards.


Technology Delivery Requests – Manage Technology Delivery Fulfillment (TDF) and Permit-to-Operate (PTO) requests as part of the release readiness process.


Observability and Monitoring – Establish and maintain observability standards for QA and production environments, including proactive monitoring, alert configurations, and performance tracking.
