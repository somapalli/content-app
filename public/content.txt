RTE Objectives & Goals

Establish a Clear Delivery Roadmap for MVP 1.2
Define and maintain a high-level delivery plan and key milestones for MVP 1.2, ensuring alignment on scope, dependencies, and target outcomes across all stakeholders.

Translate Roadmap into PI-Level Execution Plans
Break down the MVP 1.2 roadmap into well-defined epics and stories across the next three Program Increments (PIs), with clear ownership, sequencing, and dependency management to enable predictable planning.

Drive PI and Sprint Execution Excellence
Ensure consistent delivery execution within each PI by facilitating effective PI Planning, iteration planning, and ART ceremonies, and by tracking sprint-level commitments to keep teams aligned with PI objectives and overall goals.

Ensure Predictable and On-Time Delivery
Monitor progress against milestones and sprint commitments, proactively manage risks and impediments, and course-correct as needed to ensure timelines are met and delivery remains on track.

Enable Transparency and Continuous Improvement
Provide clear visibility into delivery status, risks, and dependencies through metrics and regular communication, and foster continuous improvement through Inspect & Adapt outcomes and actionable follow-ups.
