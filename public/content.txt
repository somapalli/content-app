Scepter Platform – Retail Payfac & C&IB Modernization

CAP API – Modernization, Security & Product Expansion
	•	Delivered major milestones across the CAP API modernization program, including new product launches and enhancements to existing APIs.
	•	Led the migration from JWS to JWE token encryption, executing extensive planning, customer engagement, lower-environment testing, and contingency strategies.
	•	Successfully completed two production migration legs using a wave-based approach, minimizing risk and customer impact.
	•	Led the design and architecture of Payment APIs integrated with the ICON framework, ensuring scalability, reliability, and alignment with PINACLE channels.

Business Capabilities Delivered
	•	Token Encryption & Reader API: Migrated from JWS to JWE for stronger security; enabled optional JWE decryption to support workflow parameter retrieval.
	•	ePayments API (Zelle): Modernized and integrated with the ICON Payments framework for improved performance and scale.
	•	Connected Payments API: Enabled check-print payments and manual check submission, expanding client payment options.
	•	Information Reporting API: Added support for MCA Plus and EURX accounts, balances, and transactions; included incoming low-value payments (SEPA/Bacs) in EUR and GBP.
	•	Verification Services API: Implemented POST identification/consumer KBA for stronger enrollment authentication.
	•	Virtual Card API: Added funding account token ID at payment creation and enabled inquiry by token ID.
	•	Performance Improvements: Tuned Payments, Advanced Receivables, and Information Reporting APIs to improve speed, reliability, and scalability.
